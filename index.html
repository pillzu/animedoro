Hey there! I am Piyush Upadhyay, a CS Hons. student at the University of Waterloo. The original idea was proposed by Josh Chen, a fellow youtuber whose video inspired me to build this minimalistic app for you. If you want to know why I did it, read below!

Naruto has been my ultimate craze for the past few months. The problem is that it’s sometimes hard to give it up before atleast 4-5 episodes (close to 2 hrs). Being in University right now, I can’t afford to be so lenient with my time. Therefore, using my programming skills, I built this app for all Manga/Anime fans out there. I hope this helps you focus better and finish those thrilling mangas/anime asap.
